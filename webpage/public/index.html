Version 1 was originally created for a school project and although we had brought the drone body to life, the deployment feature did not work as anticipated. The springs were not strong enough or correctly positioned to fully extend the arms and there was no mechanism to actually hold the arms in place.

Buildspace Nights & Weekends saw our first major software feature and a new spring mechanism for the drone arms.

The first thing we set out to do after getting the arms to correctly deploy was to be able to lock them in place.

Features

Rapidly Deployable

Our drone uses a collapsible, spring-powered arm mechanism to deploy and fly within seconds, while being sturdy enough in collapsed form to carry without a protective case.

Enhanced Software

With an enhanced telemetry gui and object location software, our drone is designed to maximize the amount of data that can be sent and acted upon by the user.

Affordability

Our drone diverges from the high-cost domain of drones typically priced in the tens of thousands of dollars. It is constructed using standard components found in racing-style quadcopters, and it reduces onboard weight and complexity by delegating intensive computational tasks to the ground-control systems.
